				 The project is written in JavaScript and operates entirely in a simulated environment, making it unsuitable for real-world exploitation or privilege escalation.
                 The framework was inspired by historical security research conducted on legacy macOS Mavericks (10.9), specifically related to authorization mechanisms and exploit theory.
                 Its purpose is purely academic: to help security researchers and students understand how ROP chains may be conceptually built, analyzed, and documented.

This project does not represent a real vulnerability, does not bypass system
protections, and poses no security risk to any system.
